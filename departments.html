NoMistake Medicare

NoMistakeMedicare is a service providing accurate, unbiased personalized help and advice for folks who are seeking to find the Medicare plan that is right for them.

Over the last decade, we have helped businesses, organizations and individuals take the confusion out of Medicare. Our process ensures that each person receives the personal attention they need to ensure that they make the right choice and understand why they are making that choice. As has been the case since our inception, there is never any charge for our services.
Whether you are retiring, turning 65, or evaluating your current retirement health care coverage during the Annual Election Period, we can help you select the right plan.
We provide accurate, unbiased help and advice for retired employees who are seeking to make the most of their Medicare plans.
We meet with members of churches, non-profits, unions and retirement communities to assist and guide them in choosing the right Medicare plan.
