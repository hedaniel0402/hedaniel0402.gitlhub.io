Our Services

If you want to learn more about us, you’re in the right place. Read to learn how we can help you.

Preparing to Retire Early

We support early and new retirees as you leave work and move from group to individual coverage.

Transitioning to Medicare

When you turn 65, you will be eligible to choose a Medicare plan for the first time. We can help you evaluate and select the right plan for you.

Reviewing Your Current Plan

If you’re already enrolled in a Medicare plan and are dissatisfied with your coverage, we can review your plan and provide a recommendation for a plan that will better fit your needs.

How our program works

NoMistakeMedicare simplifies the transition to Medicare coverage upon retirement. Our goal is to inform and provide individuals with the knowledge and confidence needed to make important life decisions that affect your health plan coverage. Here are some things we deliver at no cost to you!

Confidential Conversation

By signing up with NoMistakeMedicare you will be delivered timely communication in a method of your choice that is both secure and related to your personal circumstances.

Trusted Advisors

Work one-on-one with a licensed Benefit Advisor regarding your Medicare plan options. Speak with compassionate professionals who truly wants to help find the best plan for you.

Access To Health Care Plans

NoMistakeMedicare offers a wide variety of health plans, within a large scale of price ranges. We ensure you find the plan that fits for you at a budget that makes sense.


Life Lasting Support

When you work with NoMistakeMedicare regarding your retirement health insurance, we serve you through end of life! Our team provides year-round support including expert advice, help resolving claims and billing issues, assistance with annual plan renewals, and cost-savings analysis.

Resources For Your Needs

NoMistakeMedicare offers a library of information regarding health care plans, common mistakes, and things to plan for during your retirement. Also If you do not see information on something you need, let us know. We are constantly updating our resource library.

Support

NoMistakeMedicare understands that questions and life situations can arise at various times in life. We want our clients to feel comfortable contacting our experts when YOU need us.
